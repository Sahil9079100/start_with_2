# Privacy Policy – StartWith

**Effective Date:** [Add today’s date]

StartWith ("we", "our", "us") provides an AI-powered recruiting automation platform that helps organizations evaluate and sort job candidates more efficiently.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

- **Account Data:** Name, email address, organization name, and login credentials when you create an account.
- **Google Account Data:** When you connect Google Sheets or Drive, we access only the files you explicitly authorize.
- **Candidate Data:** Data contained in recruiter-uploaded Google Sheets and candidate resumes such as names, education, experience, and related job information.
- **Usage Data:** Log data, browser type, and service interaction metrics for analytics and security.

We do not collect passwords or access any data not required for functionality.

## 2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected data to:

- Access recruiter-specified Google Sheets and Drive files
- Process and analyze candidate information using AI models
- Generate candidate evaluation scores and rankings
- Display the processed results securely to the recruiter
- Maintain and improve platform performance

We do not sell, rent, or use data for advertising or marketing.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely.
- Candidate and recruiter data are processed only for the duration of analysis and stored temporarily if needed for dashboard features.
- We employ strict access controls and encryption to protect stored data.

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only when necessary:

- With Google, to access the Sheets and Drive files authorized by you.
- With service providers such as hosting or database services that are bound by confidentiality agreements.
- When required by law or legal process.

## 5. User Control

You may revoke StartWith’s access at any time through your Google Account permissions: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

To request deletion of your account or associated data, fill this form at contact.startwith.live/delete/account
We will get definetly back to you in under 24 hours

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after updates means you accept the revised policy.
